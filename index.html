Privacy Policy for FocusNest

Effective Date: 20 May 2024

Introduction

Welcome to FocusNest! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you engage with our mobile application. By accessing and using FocusNest, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy. Should you disagree with any aspect of this policy, please refrain from using our application.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

  • Email Address: Required during the signup/login process for user authentication.
  • User Data: Includes user ID, email, verification status, and account creation date.

2. Usage Data

  • Timer Data: Details of your timer activities such as activity labels, durations, and timestamps, which are stored locally on your device.

3. Device Information

  • Device Data: Information pertaining to your mobile device, including the model, operating system, and the installed version of our app.

How We Use Your Information

1. Authentication and Account Management

  • Authentication: To confirm user identity during signup/login.
  • Account Management: To administer user accounts, facilitate password resets, and enable account deletions.

2. Timer and Activity Tracking

  • Timer Functionality: To manage timer settings and operations for user activities.
  • Notifications: To alert users when a timer stops, even if the application is running in the background.
  • Data Storage: To retain completed timer data locally on your device and to present recent timers and calendar activities. Note: Your timer data is not synced across multiple devices or stored on our servers.

3. Application Improvement

  • User Feedback: To enhance app functionality and overall user experience.
  • Bug Fixes: To detect and correct software issues.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

1. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: Utilized for user authentication and to send email verifications.
  • Local Storage: Employed to store timer data directly on user devices.

2. Legal Requirements

We may release your information when legally required or when necessary to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our application.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Various protective measures are implemented to safeguard your personal information. However, it is important to acknowledge that no electronic storage or transmission method over the internet is entirely secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Your Data Rights

1. Access and Update

Users can access and modify their account information and password through the account settings.

2. Deletion

Users have the option to permanently delete their account, which also erases their data from our records.

3. Data Portability

Users can manually add and manage their timer records within the app.

Links to Other Websites

Our application may include links to third-party sites, such as our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service pages. These external sites are not operated by us. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the privacy policies of these websites. We hold no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of any linked sites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any amendments will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy within the app. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
